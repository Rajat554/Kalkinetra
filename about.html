Story

Origin Story

From a home lab to a global mission

The Beginning

KalkiNetra began as a small curiosity in a home lab in Amritsar. What started as late-night experiments in cybersecurity became a focused pursuit of understanding AI vulnerabilities.

Evolution

Over five years of dedicated research, exploration evolved into expertise. Each discovery built upon the last, creating a comprehensive understanding of AI security challenges.

The Mission Today

What started as one researcher's vision is now becoming a collective mission to understand and secure the intelligence that drives the world. We're building a community of researchers, developers, and security professionals committed to AI safety.

"Every system has a weakness. Our job is to find it before someone with malicious intent does."

— KalkiNetra Founder
