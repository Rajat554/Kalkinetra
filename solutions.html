AI Security Solutions

Tools Inspired by Research

From adversarial testing to prompt injection prevention, our upcoming tools are designed to strengthen enterprise AI security — translating cutting-edge research into practical defense systems.

At KalkiNetra, every line of research evolves into something actionable. Our solutions are not off-the-shelf products — they are research-driven prototypes, designed to help organizations anticipate, test, and mitigate AI risks before they escalate. These tools are currently under active development and pilot testing within our lab.
